# Apple with Peanut Butter

The simplest sample in the pack. Demonstrates how a recipe in the `Snacks/` folder is auto-detected as a snack (so it gets the snack icon in the meal log) without needing an explicit `type:` override.

## Ingredients
- 1 apple
- 2 tbsp peanut butter

## Instructions
1. Slice the apple.
2. Serve with peanut butter for dipping.

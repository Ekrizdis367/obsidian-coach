# Trail Mix

Make a big batch on Sunday, portion into 1/4 cup snack bags. Demonstrates the explicit `type: snack` override (would be inferred from the folder anyway, but useful when you want a recipe in a generic folder to still get the snack icon).

Per 1/4 cup serving: 210 cal / 5P / 18C / 13F.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups roasted almonds
- 1 cup cashews
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup dried cranberries
- 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
- 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds

## Instructions
1. Combine everything in a large bowl and toss.
2. Store in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks.

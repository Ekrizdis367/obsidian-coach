# Chicken Caesar Wrap

A pack-and-go lunch that uses leftover roasted or grilled chicken. Swap the wrap for a bowl of greens to skip the carbs.

## Ingredients
- 1 large flour tortilla
- 4 oz cooked chicken breast
- 2 cups romaine lettuce
- 2 tbsp caesar dressing
- 2 tbsp grated parmesan
- 1/4 cup croutons

## Instructions
1. Toss the romaine, dressing, and parmesan in a bowl.
2. Lay the tortilla flat, spread the salad down the middle, and top with chicken and croutons.
3. Fold the sides in and roll tightly. Slice in half.

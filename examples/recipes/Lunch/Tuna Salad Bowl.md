# Tuna Salad Bowl

A make-ahead lunch for two. Recipe writes nutrition as recipe totals with `servings: 2` so logging "1 serving" in Coach gives you 360 cal / 26P / 19C / 19F.

## Ingredients
- 2 cans tuna in water
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1 stalk celery
- 2 tbsp red onion
- 1 tbsp dijon mustard
- 4 cups mixed greens
- 1 avocado
- 1 lemon
- salt to taste #IgnoreIngredient
- pepper to taste #IgnoreIngredient

## Instructions
1. Drain the tuna and flake into a bowl.
2. Finely dice the celery and red onion. Add to the tuna with the mayo and mustard. Mix.
3. Squeeze in lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Divide greens between two bowls, top with tuna salad and sliced avocado.

# Pasta with Tomato Sauce

The canonical example from both plugin READMEs. Nutrition is given as recipe totals; with `servings: 4`, Coach logs 450 cal / 16P / 67C / 12F per serving.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb spaghetti
- 2 cups crushed tomatoes
- 3 cloves garlic
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup fresh basil
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- salt to taste #IgnoreIngredient
- pepper to taste #IgnoreIngredient

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti to al dente.
2. While the pasta cooks, warm the olive oil in a wide pan. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes for 30 seconds.
3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes. Simmer 8-10 minutes until thickened. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Drain the pasta, reserving a splash of pasta water. Toss the pasta with the sauce, adding pasta water if needed.
5. Top with torn basil and parmesan.

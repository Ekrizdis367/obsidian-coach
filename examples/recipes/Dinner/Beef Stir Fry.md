# Beef Stir Fry

A weeknight family-size dinner. With `servings: 4`, Coach logs 560 cal / 36P / 49C / 22F per serving.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb flank steak
- 2 cups jasmine rice
- 1 head broccoli
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 yellow onion
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 inch ginger
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Cook the rice according to package directions.
2. Slice the steak thinly against the grain. Toss with cornstarch.
3. Whisk the soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger in a small bowl.
4. Heat the oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Sear the steak in two batches, ~90 seconds per side. Remove.
5. Add broccoli florets, sliced bell pepper, and sliced onion. Stir fry 4-5 minutes.
6. Return the beef to the pan. Pour in the sauce and toss until glossy, ~1 minute.
7. Serve over rice and top with sesame seeds.

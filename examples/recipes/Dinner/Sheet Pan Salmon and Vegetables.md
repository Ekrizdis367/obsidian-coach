# Sheet Pan Salmon and Vegetables

One pan, ~25 minutes, dinner for two. Per serving: 540 cal / 38P / 32C / 28F.

## Ingredients
- 12 oz salmon fillet
- 1 lb baby potatoes
- 1 bunch asparagus
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp paprika
- salt to taste #IgnoreIngredient
- pepper to taste #IgnoreIngredient

## Instructions
1. Heat the oven to 425F.
2. Halve the potatoes and toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a sheet pan and roast 15 minutes.
3. Trim the asparagus. Toss with the remaining olive oil and minced garlic.
4. Push the potatoes to one side of the pan. Add the salmon (skin down) and asparagus. Sprinkle paprika over the salmon.
5. Roast 10-12 more minutes until the salmon flakes easily.
6. Squeeze lemon over everything before serving.

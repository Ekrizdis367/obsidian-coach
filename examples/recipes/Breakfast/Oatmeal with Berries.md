# Oatmeal with Berries

A quick stovetop breakfast that hits enough protein to hold you over until lunch.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup mixed berries
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- pinch salt #IgnoreIngredient

## Instructions
1. Combine oats, milk, and salt in a small saucepan. Bring to a low simmer.
2. Stir frequently for 4-5 minutes until thickened.
3. Top with berries, honey, and chia seeds.

# Greek Yogurt Parfait

No-cook, high-protein breakfast or post-workout snack. Layer in a glass for the look, or just stir it together in a bowl.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup plain greek yogurt
- 1/3 cup granola
- 1/2 cup mixed berries
- 1 tbsp honey

## Instructions
1. Spoon half the yogurt into a glass or bowl.
2. Add half the granola and half the berries.
3. Repeat the layers and finish with a drizzle of honey.

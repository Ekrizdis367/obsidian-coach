# Electrolyte Water

A homemade hydration drink for long workouts or hot days. Most ingredients are pantry staples you wouldn't put on a grocery list, so they're tagged with `#IgnoreIngredient` to demonstrate Grocery Planning's skip feature.

## Ingredients
- 16 oz water #IgnoreIngredient
- 1/4 tsp salt #IgnoreIngredient
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp honey
- pinch baking soda #IgnoreIngredient

## Instructions
1. Stir everything together in a tall glass or shaker bottle until dissolved.
2. Drink during or after your workout.

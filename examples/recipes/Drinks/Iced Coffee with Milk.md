# Iced Coffee with Milk

For the daily caffeine log. Logging this in your meal block as a drink keeps the macros honest if you have it more than once a day.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup brewed coffee
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 cup ice #IgnoreIngredient

## Instructions
1. Brew coffee and let cool, or brew over ice.
2. Pour over a glass of ice and add milk.

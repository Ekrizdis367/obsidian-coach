# Protein Shake

A standard post-workout shake. Folder-inferred as a `drink` so it gets the drink icon in the meal log automatically.

## Ingredients
- 1 scoop whey protein powder
- 1 cup milk
- 1 banana
- 1 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 cup ice #IgnoreIngredient

## Instructions
1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
2. Blend on high for 30-45 seconds until smooth.
